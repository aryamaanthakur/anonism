About ANONISM

None of us is perfect. We all have some hidden thoughts underlying deep inside of us which we keep holding on but at the same time we are dying to share them with everyone. It may be about mistakes we committed or some person we admire and so on. Holding on to such things only gives it more power. But confessing such emotions anonymously lightens our burden of guilt, fear or nervousness making us feel more confident and also helps us explore our deepest thoughts and feelings.
So here comes ANONISM, the anonymous confession website for the students of IIT (ISM), Dhanbad, where ISMites can confess any or every such feelings (ofcourse without hurting any other person's feelings) with 100% anonymity (i.e., not even the admins can track the user).
